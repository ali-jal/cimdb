Welcome to the 2Fast4U Computer Inventory Management Database!

2Fast4U Computers assembles and sells $2 million in PCs annually (on average 1,500 PCs assembled per year). The Computer Inventory and Manufacturing (CIM) web app helps manage our inventory, assembly, and quality control (QC) process. All 2Fast4U Employees at our production Sites have access to the database. Employees are assigned Work Orders, which list Products to assemble using Regular Components (which are available in large quantities like MotherBoard, RAM, etc.) and Special Components (CPUs, which must be tracked using unique serial numbers). Regular Components and Special Components are stored in Locations at any particular Site until they are used to assemble Products.
